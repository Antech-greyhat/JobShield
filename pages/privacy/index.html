Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

1. Information Collection

JobShield operates entirely locally on your device. We do not collect, store, or transmit your personal data, job search history, or browsing habits to our servers.

2. Local Storage

Your settings and cached verified companies are stored locally in your browser using Chrome's secure storage API.

3. Scam Reporting

When you choose to report a scam, only the URL and the description you explicitly provide are sent to our community database to help protect others.
